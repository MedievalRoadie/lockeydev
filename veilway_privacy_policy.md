# Veilway Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

## Overview

Veilway is a WireGuard VPN client application for Apple devices. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data Veilway collects (or rather, doesn't collect) and how the app operates.

## Data Collection

**Veilway does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically, we do not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Network traffic or browsing history
- VPN connection logs

## Local Data Storage

Veilway stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **VPN Configurations**: Your WireGuard tunnel configurations (server addresses, public keys, allowed IPs)
- **Private Keys**: Stored securely in the device's Keychain, protected by Apple's hardware encryption
- **App Preferences**: Your selected settings and preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

## iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your VPN configurations (excluding private keys) may be synced across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. This data is:
- Encrypted by Apple
- Stored in your private iCloud account
- Not accessible to Veilway or any third party

Private keys are never synced to iCloud and remain stored locally in each device's Keychain.

## Network Connections

When you connect to a VPN server using Veilway:
- Your traffic is routed through the WireGuard server **you configure**
- Veilway acts only as the client software
- We have no visibility into your VPN connections or traffic
- Your VPN provider's privacy policy governs the handling of your traffic

## No Backend Services

Veilway has no backend servers, user accounts, or cloud services. The app operates entirely on your device.

## Third-Party Services

Veilway does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Veilway does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
